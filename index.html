Annekeeeeeyyyy is a seasoned Art Director with over 13 years of experience in the film and television industry. A graduate of AFDA, Anneke began her career in the Costume Department, where she gained an appreciation for character development and cultivated a deep attention to detail.

She transitioned to the Art Department, where she truly found her calling. Her passion for creating immersive worlds and enhancing the narratives of stories, has made her a prominent figure in the industry.

Anneke’s ability to blend creativity with practicality has made her the professional to have on set, where her contributions continue to shape the visual landscape of contemporary storytelling.

She has contributed to over 17 drama series and 18 feature films, both locally and internationally, including Vir die Voëls, Toorbos, Liberty, Die Ellen Pakkies Storie, I Am All Girls, Hartklop, Legacy, and iNumber Number: Jozi Gold, among others.

Her impressive portfolio includes roles as Art Director on seven productions and as Production Designer on four, showcasing her versatility and creative vision. These include SAFTA-nominated and award-winning productions in her respective departments.
